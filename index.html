FAQ & Help

Q: What are the conversion rates?

A: Conversion rates are taken from crypto markets and are not altered by us in any way. You will get coins exchanged at the best price currently possible.

Q: Are there any fees?

A: Other than regular transaction fees which depend on selected cryptocurrency, FluffySwap.com takes a small 1% fee for further operation.

Q: What are the max and min amounts?

A: The maximum amount for deposit is the highest amount of coins you can get exchanged - we have a limited amount of crypto in the reserves. Minimum amount is the smallest amount that will cover the transaction fees. Your funds may get lost if you send less than the min amount.

Q: How can I reach out to you?

A: If you have questions, concerns or want to list a new coin simply e-mail us at support@fluffyswap.com - we will respond as soon as possible.
